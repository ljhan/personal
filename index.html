Heylo! I’m Alyssa Liu: front end developer, user experience designer, and all-around doer. I grew up in Naperville, IL, but I’m currently a rising junior at Northwestern University studying Computer Science and Linguistics while pursuing the Segal Design Certificate. I love creating things and experiences, whether via code or good ole pen on paper, in the kitchen or in the music studio. Every day, I’m fascinated by the ways technology touches our lives, especially when it comes to human computer interaction, augmented reality, and user experience. Over the years, I’ve worked on various hackathon projects and am currently working on the app for a security drone start up. For several months, I have also been pursuing my own startup project, Stella by Hazumi. If I’m not tinkering at the Garage or sketching, you can probably find me at the nearest boba tea place or curled up in a blanket burrito listening to music. I hope to take my ideas and aspirations beyond the Midwest - perhaps to the West coast or possibly even Japan. My dream is to bring wonder and a greater sense of connectedness to people’s lives via technology and thoughtful design. However much I dream, I aim to do.
